# TrafficGrid: First Account Registration Guide (Romania)

To ensure our first account has the highest possible "Trust Score," we will use a **Hybrid Manual-Automated** approach on your **Samsung S24 Ultra**.

## 🛠️ Step 1: Physical Preparation
1.  **SIM Card:** Insert a fresh **Orange or Digi RO** SIM card into the S24 Ultra.
2.  **Data Only:** Disable Wi-Fi. The phone **must** use the carrier's mobile data (CGNAT) to get a trusted Romanian IP.
3.  **Location:** Enable GPS. Ensure it matches your physical location in Bucharest.

## 🛠️ Step 2: The "Clean Slate"
1.  **App Reset:** Clear all cache and data for TikTok, or reinstall it.
2.  **Identity Check:** Go to `whoer.net` on the phone. Goal: 100% anonymity score.

## 🛠️ Step 3: Registration (Manual)
1.  **Method:** Sign up using the **Phone Number** from the SIM card.
2.  **Profile:** Use a natural username (e.g., `alex_dev_ro`) and a real photo.

## 🛠️ Step 4: Hand-off to Automation
Once created, connect the phone to the **HP Z420 Hub** and run the warm-up script from the MeLe.
