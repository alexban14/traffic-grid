# TrafficGrid: Romanian Market Strategy

## 🇷🇴 Target Audience: Romania
To dominate the Romanian social media space (TikTok/YouTube), our "Identity Mesh" must look and act like a local user from Bucharest, Cluj, or other major hubs.

### 1. Warm-up Interest Profiles (Niches)
For the 4 physical phones (S24 Ultra, A40, Moto G40, P10), we will rotate through these Romanian-centric interest clusters:
- **Tech & Gadgets:** #itromania #tehnologie #programare
- **Lifestyle & Comedy:** #romania #bucuresti #cluj #vibe #foryouromania
- **Business & Finance:** #afaceri #investitii #bani #antreprenoriat
- **Automotive:** #masini #trafic #auto

### 2. Behavioral Alignment
- **Timezone Sync:** All activity will be scheduled between **07:00 and 23:00 EET/EEST** (Bucharest time) to match real user patterns.
- **Geographic Signals:** 
  - **SIM Cards:** Using Orange/Digi/Vodafone RO SIMs (Mandatory).
  - **GPS Spoofing (Optional):** If needed, we will set mock locations to major Romanian cities (Sector 1, Floreasca, Pipera for high-value profiles).
- **Language:** Interactions (comments, searches) will be in Romanian or a mix of RO/EN (common for younger demographics).

### 3. Trust Building (The "Local" Factor)
- **Follow Local Creators:** We will seed the accounts by following top Romanian influencers (e.g., Selly, Dorian Popa, or tech creators like George Buhnici) to signal to the algorithm that the user is part of the Romanian social graph.
- **Local Engagement:** The bots will interact with trending Romanian hashtags to quickly get onto the "For You" page of other Romanian users.

## 🚀 Execution Goal
Within 7 days of warm-up, the "For You" feed of each device should be **>80% Romanian content**. Once achieved, the account is "Primed" for posting our own content.
